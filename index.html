How to add this to your Email client

Follow these guides to include your Email Signature to your emails.

Once you have copied your Email Signature, head over to your Email Client. Apple Mail or Microsoft Outlook

Apple Mail

  • Go to Mail/ Preferences
  • Click on the Signatures tab
  • Click on Your Signatures
  • Click on the + sign
  • Give your signature a name and then paste your copied signature into the white pane
  • Now that your signature is ready, choose it from the Choose Signature drop down
  • Start a new email and check that your Email Signature is appearing correctly

Microsoft Outlook

  • Go to Outlook/ Preferences
  • Click on the Signatures tab
  • Click on the + sign
  • Give your signature a name and then paste your copied signature into the white pane
  • Now that your signature is ready, click on Default Signature
  • Under the Default column, choose your signature and click OK
  • Start a new email and check that your Email Signature is appearing correctly
